Gear

You need skis, or snowboard. My current ski is the blizzard black pearl. You also need ski goggles, helmet, ski pants, long johns, ski jacket, gloves or mittens, ski socks, face mask (depending on the weather) The best way to find the best gear is the ski boutiques in the ski villages that seel everything you need or in a big sports store like REI.
